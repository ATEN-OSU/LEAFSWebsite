The registration process include two steps:

  1. Fill out to the google form with the applicant’s information.
  2. Upload your short video (click here for video instructions.

Please submit a short (1 min) video completing each of the activities listed below to help us get to know you so we can make this experience awesome! This helps us tailor the activities to each individual’s abilities. All applicants are welcomed into the program, regardless of ability.


We suggest completing all of the activities in one take.

  1. Introduce yourself!
  2. Remove a cap from a screw cap bottle.
  3. Plug your phone charger into a wall outlet.
  4. Write your name on a piece of paper.
  5. On a computer, open up a word processor using a mouse or trackpad and type a few sentences of your choosing.
